ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE?

We’re a team of computer and law geeks who came
together to solve one of the most frustrating problems
business owners always want to avoid: Doing their
legal policy writing themselves! Of course, you could
hire an attorney and waste valuable time and money.
Or you could just generate them on our website…
for free!

WHAT WE DO

We save you the stress of drafting complex legal
policies, such as terms and conditions and privacy
policies, by generating them for you.

WHY WE DO IT

Starting up a business is tough enough. We want you
to concentrate on the core of your business without
wasting time and money on legal jargons. That’s the
first reason. The second reason is that we love doing
those legal jargons that irritate you!
